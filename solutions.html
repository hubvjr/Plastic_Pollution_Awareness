Solutions to Plastic Pollution

Switch to reusable bags, bottles, and containers to reduce single-use plastics.

Recycle Properly

Sort and recycle plastics correctly to ensure they do not end up in oceans.

Participate in Cleanups

Join beach and river cleanup programs to remove plastics before they reach the ocean.
