About This Project

The Plastic Pollution in the Ocean Awareness Website is an educational and awareness platform created to highlight one of the most critical environmental issues affecting our planet today — plastic pollution in marine environments. The website serves as a central source of information to help people understand how plastic waste enters the ocean, why it is dangerous, and what actions can be taken to reduce its impact.
pollution has become a global problem due to the widespread use of single-use plastics and improper waste management. Every year, millions of tonnes of plastic waste are carried into the ocean through rivers, drains, coastal activities, and marine industries. Once in the ocean, plastic does not decompose naturally; instead, it breaks down into smaller fragments known as microplastics, which remain in the environment for hundreds of years and pose serious threats to marine life and ecosystems.


This website aims to educate and inform users by presenting real facts, statistics, images, and examples of how plastic pollution affects oceans, marine animals, and human health. Visitors can learn about the causes of ocean plastic pollution, such as littering, poor recycling systems, and excessive plastic production, as well as the harmful effects including animal entanglement, ingestion of plastic, habitat destruction, and contamination of the food chain.
In addition to raising awareness, the website focuses on solutions and positive action. It encourages responsible plastic use, waste reduction, recycling, and the adoption of eco-friendly alternatives. The platform also promotes participation in cleanup activities, community programs, and global campaigns aimed at protecting marine environments.


Mission
The mission of the Plastic Pollution in the Ocean Awareness Website is to inspire individuals, communities, and organizations to take responsibility for their plastic consumption and actively contribute to protecting the ocean. By increasing awareness and sharing practical solutions, the website seeks to drive meaningful behavioral change.

Vision
The vision of this website is a future where oceans are clean, healthy, and free from plastic pollution — a world where marine ecosystems thrive and future generations can enjoy safe, sustainable, and beautiful oceans.
